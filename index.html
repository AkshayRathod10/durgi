About

Find Out More About Us

Durgi immigration is a trusted Immigration Advisor and Consultant for Canada and Australia Immigration, Permanent Residency (PR) process. we offer expert immigration advice, assistance, and support to the people applying for PR Visa, Skilled visa, Study visa, Family sponsored visa, etc. in Canada, Australia, Portugal, Sweden, etc. countries.

With extensive years of experience in the immigration industry, today, Durgi immigration is a trusted name, when it comes to providing seamless visa solutions and services.

As a prominent Visa Consultant for Canada and Australia, We believes in client service with utmost transparency and ethical approach. Durgi immigration specialist has an excellent success rate of over 95 percent for obtaining the Canada and Australia PR visa approvals.

Our team offers key services to the individuals applying visa in different countries, i.e.

  • Application pre-assessment- Qualified immigration experts perform free assessment of your profile confirming the eligibility status
  • Selection of right visa program- we help you choose right immigration program to apply and creating online profile of applicant with submission of Expression of Interest (EOI).
  • Documentation Assistance– Helping applicants know the details of required visa documents and pathways to obtain the same.
  • Language coaching- coaching to prepare the students for the language tests, i.e. IELTS exam.
  • Visa Application Filing- Helping the applicants file visa application accurately with zero error
  • Application Follow up- It's crucial to follow up the visa application with immigration office to win timely approval
